James Clear recently asked, "If your family inherited only your
habits ..., which ones would be your richest gifts?"[^jc]
I was struck by the question, and not in a good way.
The reality is that my "habits" recently consist of being distant
and grumpy.
Sure, I have my own stuff going on, but I have done a poor job
insulating my family from it.
